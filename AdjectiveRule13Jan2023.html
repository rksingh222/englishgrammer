adjective - An adjective modifies or gives more information about a noun or pronoun
an adjective tell about the quality, quantity, number or order of the noun that it describes
Adjective RuleExample
Adjective of quality(explains what kind)the hungry fox chased a cat
Adjective of quantity(explains how much and mostly describe uncountable noun)there is a little water to drink
Adjective of number(explains how many)i will be absent for 6 days(explains in what order)I stood in 3rd row
an adjective used with uncountable noun is an adjective of qualityi have some food
an adjective used with countable noun is an adjective of numberi have some dishes
an adjective may also come immediately beside the noun it describesthe heat made the child sleepy
an adjective can also be placed after the verb to bethis palace is huge
