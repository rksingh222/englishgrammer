When an adverb modifies a verb, it can often be categorized as one of the following:
TypeExample
Adverb of manner(how)Adverb of manner tells us how an action occurs - the lion crawled stelathily
Adverb of time(when) or (how often)Adverb of time tells when an action occurs - the lion purss at 10:30 am
Adverb of place(where)Adverb of place tells where an action occurs - poetry surrounds us everywhere
Adverb of degree(aka adverb of comparision or how much)Adverb of degree tells us to what degree an action occurs- he works smarter
